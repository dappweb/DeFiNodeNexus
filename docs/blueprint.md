# **App Name**: DeFi Node Nexus

## Core Features:

- Wallet Connect & Asset Overview: Securely connect to a user's crypto wallet and display current balances for TOT, TOF, and all associated NFTs (NFTA & NFTB).
- NFTA Node Management: View details of owned NFTA nodes, monitor accrued daily TOT yield, and facilitate the withdrawal process which requires TOF consumption.
- NFTB Dividend Tracking: Display earned NFTB dividends, including both TOT and USDT, with detailed historical distribution records based on the NFTB node's level and weight.
- Prediction Platform Integration: Provide a clear interface to view real-time statistics and historical performance from the connected prediction platform, and enable basic user interaction to participate.
- Tokenomics Dashboard: Visualize key tokenomics parameters for TOT and TOF, offering insights into distribution, transaction fees, and the deflationary mechanisms.
- AI-Powered Yield Insight Tool: An AI tool to analyze user's NFT holdings, token balances, and prediction market participation history, suggesting tailored strategies to optimize TOT/TOF yield and potential earnings.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual design will adopt a dark scheme, inspired by themes of blockchain innovation, digital finance, and precision analytics. The primary color is a deep, trustworthy blue (`#1A72FF`), signifying stability and advanced technology. The background color is a very dark, subtly blue-tinged grey (`#16181B`), providing a sophisticated backdrop. An accent color of bright cyan (`#0CDBFD`) will be used to highlight key interactive elements and provide visual alerts.
- Headlines and prominent text will use 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its modern, slightly futuristic appeal, reinforcing the app's tech-forward identity. Body text and data displays will utilize 'Inter' (sans-serif), ensuring excellent readability for complex information and extensive numerical data.
- Employ a suite of clean, minimalistic SVG icons. These icons will draw inspiration from blockchain technology, financial charts, and prediction symbols, maintaining a cohesive and modern digital aesthetic.
- The application will feature a dashboard-centric layout, optimized for clear presentation of complex financial data. Information will be organized into modular, distinct panels, ensuring users can easily access and interpret key metrics, asset performance, and actionable insights.
- Subtle, smooth animations will be incorporated for UI interactions, such as data updates, navigation transitions, and hover effects. These animations will enhance user experience without causing distraction, reinforcing the feeling of a dynamic and responsive platform.
